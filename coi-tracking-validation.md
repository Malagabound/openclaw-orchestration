# Certificate of Insurance (COI) Tracking SaaS - Phase 1 Validation

**Date**: February 9, 2026  
**Researcher**: George  
**Product Type**: Micro-SaaS (B2B Subscription)  

## Quick Assessment

**Target Market**: Small-to-medium general contractors (1-10 employees) who need to track insurance certificates from subcontractors.

**Core Problem**: GCs are legally/contractually required to maintain current insurance certificates from all subs. Manual tracking via Excel/email leads to expired coverage, compliance violations, and liability exposure.

## Market Analysis

### The Pain Point
- **Legal Requirement**: Most contracts require GCs to verify sub insurance
- **Liability Risk**: Working with uninsured subs = massive legal exposure  
- **Manual Process**: Excel spreadsheets, email follow-ups, paper files
- **Time Consuming**: Chasing subs for renewals, organizing documents
- **Compliance Stress**: Audits, inspections, permit requirements

### Target Customer Profile
- **Size**: 1-10 employee general contractors
- **Focus**: Residential/light commercial construction
- **Pain**: "I spend hours every month chasing insurance certificates"
- **Budget**: $50-150/month to avoid legal problems
- **Geography**: Every US market (construction is local everywhere)

### Current Solutions (Preliminary)
Based on market knowledge:

**Enterprise Tier** ($300+/month):
- Procore, Buildertrend, CoConstruct
- **Gap**: Overkill for small GCs, expensive, complex

**Mid-Market** ($100-300/month):  
- Some specialized COI tracking tools exist
- **Gap**: Still too complex/expensive for small operators

**Small Business** (<$100/month):
- **MAJOR GAP**: Almost nothing affordable for 1-10 person GCs
- Most use Excel + email (manual hell)

## Business Model Analysis

### Revenue Model
- **Monthly SaaS**: $50-150/month per GC
- **Annual discount**: 2 months free (common in construction)
- **Add-ons**: Document storage, custom reports

### Market Sizing
- **Total GCs in US**: ~700,000 general contractors
- **Target segment**: 1-10 employees = ~400,000 GCs  
- **Serviceable market**: 5% adoption = 20,000 customers
- **Revenue potential**: 20,000 × $100/month = $2M ARR ceiling

### Unit Economics
- **ARPU**: $100/month ($1,200/year)
- **CAC**: ~$300 (Google Ads, local marketing)
- **LTV**: 24 months × $100 = $2,400
- **LTV:CAC**: 8:1 (excellent)

## Product Concept

### Core Features (MVP)
1. **Sub Database**: Contact info, trade, insurance details
2. **Document Storage**: Upload/store COI certificates  
3. **Expiration Tracking**: Visual dashboard of renewal dates
4. **Automated Alerts**: Email/SMS before expiration
5. **Compliance Reports**: Simple reports for audits

### Advanced Features
- Integration with common construction management tools
- Sub portal (subs can update their own certificates)
- Custom insurance requirement templates
- Mobile app for job site verification

### Differentiation Strategy
- **Simplicity**: Dead simple compared to enterprise tools
- **Price**: $50-100/month vs $300+ for alternatives
- **Niche Focus**: Built specifically for small GCs, not general PM
- **Local Marketing**: Target local contractor associations
