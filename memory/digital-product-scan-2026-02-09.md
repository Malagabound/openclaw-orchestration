## Emerging Opportunities (New Discoveries)

### 1. AI Workflow Automation Templates
**Gap Identified**: While AI tools exist, most people don't know how to chain them together efficiently.

- **Product**: Notion templates with AI workflow automations for specific professions
- **Examples**: "Social Media Manager's AI Toolkit", "Content Creator's AI Pipeline", "Freelancer's AI Assistant Setup"
- **Price Point**: $47-97 (bundle format)
- **Target**: Professionals who use AI but struggle with workflow optimization
- **Evidence**: High search volume for "AI automation" + "productivity templates"

### 2. Micro-SaaS Teardown Templates
**Gap Identified**: People want to build micro-SaaS but don't know what features to include.

- **Product**: Detailed breakdown templates of successful micro-SaaS products
- **Format**: Notion database + analysis framework for features, pricing, positioning
- **Price Point**: $67-127
- **Target**: Indie builders, developers wanting to build SaaS
- **Evidence**: High interest in micro-SaaS content, but mostly high-level advice

### 3. Client Red Flag Checklists for Service Providers
**Gap Identified**: Service providers repeatedly get burned by bad clients.

- **Product**: Industry-specific checklists to screen clients before taking them on
- **Examples**: "Graphic Designer's Client Red Flags", "Web Developer Warning Signs"
- **Format**: PDF checklists + email templates for handling difficult situations
- **Price Point**: $27-47
- **Target**: Freelancers, consultants, service providers
- **Evidence**: Constant complaints in freelancer communities about bad clients

### 4. Platform Migration Toolkits
**Gap Identified**: When platforms change (Twitter → X, Instagram algorithm updates), creators panic.

- **Product**: Step-by-step migration guides + content templates for moving audiences
- **Examples**: "Twitter to Bluesky Migration Kit", "Instagram to TikTok Content Adapter"
- **Price Point**: $37-67
- **Target**: Content creators, social media managers
- **Evidence**: Platform instability creates urgent need for backup plans
