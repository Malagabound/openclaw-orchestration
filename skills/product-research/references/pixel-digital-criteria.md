# Pixel Digital Product Scoring Criteria

Crystal clear standards for evaluating digital product opportunities (templates, tools, courses, assets).

## 🚨 REGULATED/COMPLIANCE INDUSTRIES - AUTO-REJECT (IRONCLAD)

**IMMEDIATE RED FLAG KEYWORDS - DO NOT RESEARCH:**
- **regulated, compliance, regulatory, government, EPA, FDA, OSHA, HIPAA, SOX, PCI**
- **medical, healthcare, pharmaceutical, finance, banking, legal, insurance**
- **trucking regulations, medical waste, lab animal care, environmental compliance**
- **prompt engineering, AI prompts, prompt chains** (saturated 2022-era market)

**IF ANY OF THESE APPEAR → STOP IMMEDIATELY. DO NOT SCORE. MOVE ON.**

## 1. DEMAND (1-5)

**5 - Exceptional:**
- 50k+ monthly searches for product type
- Top products in category earning $10k+ monthly
- Viral social media content around need
- Active buyer communities (Facebook groups, Discord servers)

**4 - Strong:**
- 10k-50k monthly searches
- Top products earning $3k-10k monthly  
- Regular social media discussion
- Engaged communities requesting this type of product

**3 - Moderate:**
- 2k-10k monthly searches
- Top products earning $1k-3k monthly
- Some social media interest
- Basic communities/forums discussing need

**2 - Weak:**
- 500-2k monthly searches
- Top products earning $300-1k monthly
- Limited social discussion
- Niche interest with small audience

**1 - None:**
- <500 monthly searches
- Top products earning <$300 monthly
- No social media discussion
- Market seems non-existent

## 2. COMPETITION (1-5)

**5 - Blue Ocean:**
- No direct products available
- Adjacent products are expensive/complex
- Clear gap in marketplace
- First-mover advantage possible

**4 - Light Competition:**
- 1-5 direct products
- Existing products have poor reviews/design
- Clear improvement opportunities
- Room for better execution

**3 - Moderate Competition:**
- 5-15 products available
- Mix of quality in existing products
- Market large enough for multiple winners
- Differentiation opportunities exist

**2 - Heavy Competition:**
- 15-50 products available
- Some high-quality established products
- Market becoming saturated
- Differentiation challenging

**1 - Saturated:**
- 50+ products available
- Multiple top-selling, well-designed products
- Race to bottom pricing
- No clear differentiation possible

## 3. MONETIZATION (1-5)

**5 - Excellent ($5k+ potential monthly):**
- Premium business market ($100+ price point)
- B2B/professional buyers
- High perceived value
- Upsell opportunities (bundles, variations)

**4 - Strong ($2k-5k potential monthly):**
- Professional market ($50-100 price point)
- Small business buyers
- Good perceived value
- Some bundle opportunities

**3 - Moderate ($1k-2k potential monthly):**
- Prosumer market ($20-50 price point)
- Individual professionals/creators
- Adequate perceived value
- Limited expansion opportunities

**2 - Weak ($300-1k potential monthly):**
- Hobbyist market ($10-20 price point)
- Individual consumers
- Price-sensitive buyers
- No upsell potential

**1 - Poor (<$300 potential monthly):**
- Mass consumer market (<$10 price point)
- Extreme price sensitivity
- Commodity perception
- Downward pricing pressure

## 4. BUILDABILITY (1-5)

**5 - Very Easy (1-3 days):**
- Simple template/design work
- Uses existing skills/tools
- No custom development required
- Quick iteration possible

**4 - Easy (1 week):**
- Moderate design complexity
- Some new tools/techniques to learn
- Mostly template-based work
- Straightforward execution

**3 - Moderate (2-3 weeks):**
- Complex design requirements
- Multiple file formats/variations needed
- Some custom development
- Moderate technical challenges

**2 - Hard (1-2 months):**
- Advanced design/development skills required
- Custom tools/software needed
- Significant learning curve
- Complex technical requirements

**1 - Very Hard (3+ months):**
- Expert-level skills required
- Cutting-edge tools/techniques
- High probability of failure
- Major technical unknowns

## 5. SCALABILITY (1-5)

**5 - Fully Automated:**
- Digital delivery only
- No customer support needed
- Instant download/access
- Zero marginal costs

**4 - Mostly Automated:**
- Minimal support required
- Standard FAQ handling
- Occasional customer queries
- Near-zero marginal costs

**3 - Semi-Automated:**
- Some customer support needed
- Installation/usage guidance required
- Moderate time investment per sale
- Low ongoing costs

**2 - Labor Intensive:**
- Significant customer support
- Custom modifications requested
- High time investment per customer
- Moderate ongoing costs

**1 - Not Scalable:**
- Heavy customization required
- Extensive hand-holding needed
- Time-intensive per customer
- High support burden

## 6. PASSION FIT (1-5)

**5 - Perfect Fit:**
- Real estate/property templates
- Crypto/blockchain designs
- Software development tools
- Personal expertise area

**4 - Strong Fit:**
- Business/productivity templates
- Tech-related designs
- Professional services tools
- Adjacent to expertise

**3 - Moderate Fit:**
- Generic business templates
- Standard design patterns
- No special knowledge required
- Could learn quickly

**2 - Weak Fit:**
- Outside comfort zone
- Requires design skills development
- Limited personal interest
- Steep learning curve

**1 - Poor Fit:**
- Completely foreign domain
- No personal interest/use
- Major skill gaps
- Low probability of quality execution
