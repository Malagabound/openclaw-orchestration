## Scope: AI-Automatable Business Acquisitions

**Target Profile:**
- **Price Range:** $25K-$150K acquisition cost
- **Multiple:** 2-6x annual profit (4x SDE ideal)
- **Business Type:** Online/digital businesses with automation potential
- **Profit:** Documented profitability for 12+ months
- **Management:** Can run without daily hands-on involvement

**Avoid:**
- Physical/brick-mortar businesses
- High-touch service businesses
- Inventory-heavy operations
- Single-customer dependencies (>30% revenue)
- Declining industries

### Due Diligence Research

**Financial Verification:**
- Revenue documentation (Stripe/PayPal screenshots)
- Profit & loss statements (minimum 12 months)
- Traffic analytics (Google Analytics access)
- Customer retention metrics
- Churn rates and growth trends

**Operational Assessment:**
- Team structure and dependencies
- Technology stack and maintenance requirements
- Customer support burden
- Legal compliance status
- Intellectual property ownership

**Market Position:**
- Competitive landscape analysis
- Market growth trends
- Customer feedback and reviews
- Pricing power and elasticity
- Expansion opportunities

## Automation Opportunity Analysis

**High Automation Potential:**
- API-based businesses
- Content management platforms
- Data processing services
- Template/asset marketplaces
- Educational content delivery

**Medium Automation Potential:**
- Customer support can be systematized
- Sales processes can be automated
- Content creation can be templated
- User onboarding can be streamlined

**Low Automation Potential (Avoid):**
- Custom service delivery
- High-touch consulting
- Manual content creation
- Complex customer success needs

## Risk Assessment Framework

**Financial Risks:**
- Revenue concentration (customer dependency)
- Seasonal/cyclical revenue patterns
- Margin compression trends
- Working capital requirements

**Operational Risks:**
- Key person dependencies (founder, lead developer)
- Technology obsolescence
- Regulatory/compliance changes
- Competitive threats

**Market Risks:**
- Industry decline or disruption
- Platform dependencies (Google, Facebook, etc.)
- Economic sensitivity
- Substitute products/services

## Integration Planning

**Immediate (0-30 days):**
- Secure all accounts and access
- Document all processes and systems
- Identify automation opportunities
- Set up monitoring and analytics

**Short-term (30-90 days):**
- Implement quick automation wins
- Optimize pricing and operations
- Reduce manual processes
- Improve customer experience

**Long-term (90+ days):**
- Scale automation systems
- Expand revenue streams
- Optimize for passive income
- Consider additional acquisitions

## Valuation Guidelines

**SaaS Businesses:**
- 3-6x Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)
- Higher multiples for growing, lower churn
- Premium for strong competitive moats

**Content/Affiliate Businesses:**
- 24-36x monthly profit
- Higher multiples for diversified traffic
- Discount for Google/platform dependency

**E-commerce/Service Businesses:**
- 2-4x annual profit
- Higher multiples for recurring customers
- Premium for strong brand/moats

**Due Diligence Checklist:**
- [ ] 12+ months financial records verified
- [ ] Traffic/revenue sources documented  
- [ ] Customer concentration analyzed
- [ ] Competition assessed
- [ ] Legal/IP issues cleared
- [ ] Automation opportunities identified
- [ ] Integration plan developed
- [ ] ROI projections calculated

## Success Metrics

**Post-Acquisition Tracking:**
- Monthly profit margins
- Customer acquisition cost
- Customer lifetime value
- Automation percentage (manual vs automated tasks)
- Time required for management (goal: <5 hours/week)
- Return on investment (target: 25%+ annually)
